Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Privacy First Commitment: Auto Expense is designed with privacy as the core principle. All your financial data is processed locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Profile picture (if available)
  • Google account ID for authentication

Transaction Data: All transaction information is processed and stored locally on your device, including:

  • SMS and email notifications (read-only access)
  • Manually entered transaction details
  • Categories and payment methods you configure

2. How We Use Your Information

Google Account Data: Used solely for:

  • User authentication and app access
  • Displaying your name and profile in the app interface
  • No other processing or sharing occurs

Transaction Data: Processed entirely on your device to:

  • Parse and categorize your expenses automatically
  • Generate spending insights and reports
  • Provide personalized financial summaries

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: All your financial data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage mechanisms provided by your operating system.

No Cloud Storage: We do not store any of your transaction data, financial information, or personal details on our servers or any third-party cloud services.

Biometric Security: When enabled, biometric authentication data is handled entirely by your device's secure enclave and never accessed by our app.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. This includes:

  • No analytics or tracking services
  • No advertising networks
  • No data brokers or marketing companies
  • No government agencies (unless legally required)

5. Permissions We Request

SMS Access: Read-only access to parse transaction notifications from banks and payment services. We never send SMS messages.

Biometric Authentication: Access to Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint sensors for secure app access.

Notifications: Permission to send you daily spending summaries and budget alerts.

6. Google OAuth Integration

We use Google OAuth for secure authentication. This integration:

  • Follows Google's security and privacy standards
  • Only requests minimal necessary permissions (profile, email)
  • Allows you to revoke access at any time through your Google account settings

7. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you:

  • Manually delete the app
  • Clear app data through device settings
  • Use the logout function (which clears local authentication data)

8. Children's Privacy

Auto Expense is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
